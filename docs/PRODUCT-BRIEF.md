# Dikho — Product Brief

> Mirror of `plan.md` Section A. Single source of truth for what we are building.
> Update this file when the brief changes — never the code comments.

See [plan.md](../plan.md) Section A for the full brief.

**TL;DR:** Dikho gives Indian local-service businesses (salons, dentists, clinics, tutors, vets) a WhatsApp-built website + Google Business Profile + WhatsApp booking + reviews. Free until real customers arrive, then ₹199–₹499/month to unlock lead details.

**Mission:** "Google pe Dikho. Customers tak Pahuncho."

**Core moat:** Lead-lock paywall — owner sees "1 new customer tried to book" → pays to unlock.

**Launch:** Salons + spas in Chandigarh, Month 1.
